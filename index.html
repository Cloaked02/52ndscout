Who We Are

Registered 27th October 1925, We are a Scout group that consists of Beavers, Cubs, Scouts and a District Explorer unit.

We are based at the Reading Scout and Guide HQ. Northcourt Road, Reading, RG2 7HG

What We Do

Scouts is where young people make new friends, have amazing adventures, and learn new skills.
